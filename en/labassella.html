BASSELLA EXPERIENCE

AREA 1

Off-road circuit delimited from three hectares with basic artificial obstacles and with all the necessary elements to start the best form in driving 4x4.

AREA 2

Circuit with very concentrated natural obstacles and followed together. 6 hectares and about 10km travel. It contains narrow slopes with different inclination angles delimited by trees and rocks.

AREA 3

Offroad circuit with more than 20 hectares of surface, ideal for practicing 4x4, enduro, rally driving, ATV and Side by Side. It is a forest with natural obstacles of all levels.

RAPID TRACK

Steam of land created with the aim of testing your vehicle and enjoying a faster driving combined with more technical sections.

PIER

On the banks of the Rialb swamp, it allows to offer complementary aquatic activities, as well as canoeing, kayaking, etc.

BASE EDIFICE

Its structure is very practical, designed so that its use is multifunctional. It has a workshop with elevator, meeting rooms, wcs, showers and changing rooms.
