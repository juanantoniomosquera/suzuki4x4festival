WHAT IS SUZUKI 4x4 FESTIVAL?

NATURE

Enjoy three days of camping in a unique enclave located in the middle of nature on the banks of the Rialb swamp.

ADRENALINE

Explain yourself and take the maximum potential from your 4x4 Suzuki within the Off-Road world.

Suzuki 4x4 Festival is a sporting event designed in order to bring together all the owners, followers or fans of the SUV vehicle vehicles of the Suzuki brand. The first edition of the first annual and European event that will bring together users exclusively from this brand. And you will be present!

PLANNING

PRESENTATION NEW SUZUKI 4X4

Suzuki will take advantage of the occasion and present an exclusive model Suzuki 4x4.

4X4 TRACKS

4x4 outputs will be made in reduced groups and all levels taking advantage of all natural extension and available circuits.

TRAINING AND TALKS

We will have several formations in situ (correct use of winters, driving tips, desatante maneuvers..)

OFF-ROAD PREPARATORS

Throughout the event our professional 4x4 trainers will make off-road preparations live.

CHILDREN'S ACTIVITIES

We know that passion for offroad is lived since childhood, so we will have different childhood activities so that children, together with their parents, can enjoy the event.
